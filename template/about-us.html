About Us

DG REALSTATE is an online real estate marketplace that connects people who desire to rent/buy properties with real estate agents, developers and homeowners for listed inventory in locations of choice. We are creating a robust ecosystem that enables Agents, Developers, Users, Regulators and the general public to engage effectively.

Our purpose is to make buying and renting of properties a happier, easier and faster experience. We do this by listening to the needs of all players in the ecosystem and leveraging technology to provide solutions that meet these needs.

We are a team of progressive young professional based in Benin City, Nigeria. Our platform is accessible via website.

Our Mission & Vision

Our vision

To be the number one go to e-commerce real estate platform for property searchers and investors around the world

Our mission

To build trust and convenience in all types of real estate investments for all clients.
